# Mamantilie-App
Ultimate food app for Mamantilie and other street food vendors
The App main goal is to Revolutionalize the food sector in Tanzania and beyond.
Main focus is to utilize data and help food vendors make better decision to impact food waste management
Other objectives is to give street food vendors access to resources like Finance, Loans, Materials and Services.
Impact expected Cleaner environment, Larger Profit margines, more digital integration of people.
